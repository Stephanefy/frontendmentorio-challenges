Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of our FAQs. If you have any other questions you'd like answered please feel free to email us.

  • What is a Bookmark?

    A bookmark is a link to a web page that can be saved for future reference. It is a way to save a web page in your browser for easy access to it later.

  • How can I request a new browser?

    Chrome and Firefox are the most popular browsers at the moment. If you've got a favourite you'd like us to prioritize, please let us know by emailing us.

  • Is there a mobile app?

    Our app is available on all mobile platforms. You can download it at the App Store or Google Play.

  • Can I use Bookmarks on more than one device?

    Yes, you can access your Bookmarks on any device. Our app is available on all mobile platforms. You can download it at the App Store or Google Play.
